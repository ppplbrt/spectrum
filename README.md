# *Spectrum* — From Ghost to Phenomenon / Da fantasma a fenomeno

A history of the Latin word *spectrum* from Cicero to Newton, tracing its semantic trajectory across seventeen centuries — from Epicurean philosophy to Christian demonology, from humanist lexicography to seventeenth-century optics.

Storia della parola latina *spectrum* da Cicerone a Newton, attraverso diciassette secoli di filosofia epicurea, demonologia cristiana, lessicografia umanistica e ottica seicentesca.

## The interactive chart / La mappa interattiva

The chart maps the word *spectrum* along two axes: time (horizontal) and the natural–supernatural polarity (vertical). Each node represents a primary source. The vertical position reflects the meaning that *that specific text* assigns to the word — not the cultural climate of the period.

Click any node to open a detail panel with the source, date, and a note on why the node is positioned where it is.
